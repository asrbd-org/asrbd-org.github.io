# About ASRBD

![ASRBD Cybersecurity Bangladesh](/assets/img/asrbd-preview.png)

**Advanced Security Researchers Bangladesh (ASRBD)** is a non-profit cybersecurity organization dedicated to strengthening digital safety, awareness, and ethical hacking culture across Bangladesh.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations in Bangladesh with the knowledge and tools required to defend against modern cyber threats, promote ethical hacking, and build a safer digital ecosystem.

---

## What We Do

- 🔐 Cybersecurity Awareness Programs  
- 🧠 Ethical Hacking & Training  
- 🛡️ Digital Safety Campaigns  
- 🧪 Research on Cyber Threats  
- 🤝 Community Building for Security Enthusiasts  

---

## Why ASRBD

- Focused on Bangladesh's cybersecurity landscape  
- Community-driven and research-oriented  
- Promoting ethical hacking responsibly  
- Supporting future cybersecurity professionals  

---

## Our Impact

- Educating individuals on cybersecurity basics  
- Building an active cybersecurity community  
- Publishing educational blogs and resources  

---

## Our Team

ASRBD is built by passionate cybersecurity researchers, developers, and ethical hackers committed to protecting the digital space.
