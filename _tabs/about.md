# About ASRBD

![ASRBD Cybersecurity Bangladesh](/assets/img/asrbd-preview.png)

**Advanced Security Researchers Bangladesh (ASRBD)** is a non-profit cybersecurity organization committed to advancing **cybersecurity awareness, ethical hacking, and digital safety in Bangladesh**.

We aim to build a **secure digital future** by empowering individuals, students, and professionals with the knowledge and skills needed to defend against modern cyber threats.

---

## 🌐 Our Mission

Our mission is to:

- Strengthen **cybersecurity awareness in Bangladesh**
- Promote **ethical hacking and responsible security research**
- Develop a **skilled and ethical cyber force**
- Support individuals and organizations in protecting digital assets
- Contribute to a **safer and more resilient digital ecosystem**

---

## 🚀 What We Do

At ASRBD, we actively work on:

- 🔐 **Cybersecurity Awareness Campaigns**  
- 🧑‍💻 **Ethical Hacking Training & Learning Resources**  
- 🛡️ **Digital Safety & Privacy Education**  
- 🧪 **Cyber Threat Research & Analysis**  
- 🤝 **Community Building for Security Enthusiasts in Bangladesh**

---

## 🇧🇩 Why ASRBD Matters

- Focused on **Bangladesh’s real-world cybersecurity challenges**
- Driven by a **community-first approach**
- Promoting **ethical hacking with responsibility and integrity**
- Supporting the **next generation of cybersecurity professionals**
- Bridging the gap between **awareness and practical skills**

---

## 📊 Our Impact

- Educating individuals on **cybersecurity fundamentals and best practices**
- Building a growing **cybersecurity community in Bangladesh**
- Publishing **educational content, blogs, and awareness materials**
- Encouraging responsible participation in **bug bounty and security research**
