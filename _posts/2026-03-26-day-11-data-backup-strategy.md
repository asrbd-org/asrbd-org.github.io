## 💾 Data Backup Strategies

**“No backup = No mercy. One attack can erase everything.”**

Data loss isn’t a question of *if*—it’s *when*. Whether it’s ransomware, accidental deletion, device failure, or hacking, your data is always at risk.

That’s why having a solid backup strategy is not optional—it’s essential.

---

## 🔐 What is the 3-2-1 Backup Rule?

The most effective way to protect your data:

- **3 Copies** → Keep at least three copies of your data  
- **2 Different Media** → Store on different devices (e.g., PC + external drive)  
- **1 Offsite Copy** → Use cloud or remote storage  

---

## ⚡ Best Practices for Data Backup

- 💻 Use both **local and cloud storage**
- 🔄 Enable **automatic backups**
- 🔒 Keep backups **encrypted and secure**
- 🧪 **Test your backups** regularly
- 🚫 Never rely on a single backup source

---

## 🚨 Why Backup Matters

Cybercriminals often target and delete backups first during attacks like ransomware. Without a secure backup, recovery becomes nearly impossible.

Backup is your **last line of defense**.
