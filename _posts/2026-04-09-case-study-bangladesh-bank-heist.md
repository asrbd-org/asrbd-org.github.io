## 💰 The $81 Million Cyber Heist

In 2016, hackers stole **$81 million** from Bangladesh Bank using the SWIFT system.

This is known as the Bangladesh Bank Heist — one of the biggest cyberattacks in history.

---

## 🔍 How It Happened

- Malware infection  
- Weak internal security  
- Unauthorized SWIFT transactions  
- Log manipulation to hide traces  

---

## ⚠️ Key Lessons

### 1. No One Is Immune
Even central banks can be hacked.

### 2. Human Error Is Critical
Phishing emails can open doors.

### 3. Monitoring Is Essential
Attackers stayed undetected for weeks.

### 4. Secure Core Systems
Critical systems must be isolated.

---

## 🛡️ How to Stay Safe

- Avoid suspicious emails  
- Update systems regularly  
- Enable multi-factor authentication  
- Monitor unusual activities  

---

## 🚨 Final Thought

Cybersecurity is not optional.

Learn from real attacks — before becoming a victim.
