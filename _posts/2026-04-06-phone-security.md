## 📱 Protect Your Phone

Your phone is more than a device.

It holds your personal, financial, and digital identity.

---

## ⚠️ Why It Matters

Your phone contains:
- Emails  
- Social media  
- Banking apps  
- Private data  

If compromised, everything is at risk.

---

## 🔐 Use Strong Lock

- Set a strong PIN or password  
- Enable fingerprint or face unlock  

Avoid simple or predictable locks.

---

## 🔄 Keep It Updated

- Install system updates  
- Update apps regularly  

Updates fix security vulnerabilities.

---

## 📥 Install Trusted Apps Only

- Use official app stores  
- Avoid unknown APK files  

Untrusted apps may contain malware.

---

## 🔑 Manage Permissions

- Review app permissions  
- Remove unnecessary access  

Apps should only access what they need.

---

## 📡 Avoid Public Wi-Fi Risks

- Avoid logging into sensitive accounts  
- Use VPN if necessary  

Public networks are not secure.

---

## 🔔 Enable Security Features

- Enable device tracking  
- Turn on remote lock/wipe  
- Use security alerts  

---

## 🧠 Final Thoughts

Your phone is your digital life.

Protecting it means protecting everything.

---

## 🚀 Stay Secure

Take action today.

Follow ASRBD (Advanced Security Researchers Bangladesh) for more cybersecurity awareness.

Stay aware. Stay secure.
