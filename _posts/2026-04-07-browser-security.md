## 🚨 Your Browser Is Watching You

Your browser stores more data about you than you think.

Every website you visit leaves traces through cookies, trackers, and scripts. While some are useful, many are designed to monitor your behavior — and even expose you to cyber threats.

---

## ⚠️ What Happens Behind the Scenes?

When you browse:

- Cookies store login sessions and preferences  
- Trackers follow your activity across websites  
- Scripts analyze your behavior in real time  

This data can be used to:
- Build detailed profiles about you  
- Launch targeted phishing attacks  
- Deliver malicious advertisements  

---

## 🎯 Real Risk Scenario

You search for a product →  
Visit multiple websites →  
Trackers log your activity →  
You see a “perfect” ad →  
You click and enter your details →  
💥 Your data is compromised  

---

## 🛡️ How to Secure Your Browser

### 1. Clear Cookies Regularly
Removes stored tracking data and session info.

### 2. Use Privacy Extensions
Install:
- Ad blockers  
- Tracker blockers  
- Script control tools  

### 3. Use a Secure Browser
Choose browsers with built-in privacy protection.

### 4. Disable Third-Party Cookies
Prevents cross-site tracking.

### 5. Always Check HTTPS
Avoid entering sensitive data on non-secure websites.
