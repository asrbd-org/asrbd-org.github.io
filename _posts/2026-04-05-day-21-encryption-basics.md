## 🔐 Encryption Basics — Protect Your Data Before Attackers See It

Encryption is one of the strongest foundations of cybersecurity. It transforms readable data into coded information that only authorized users can unlock.

Whether you're using a laptop, smartphone, or cloud storage, encryption helps protect your data from theft, leaks, and unauthorized access.

### Why encryption matters
- Protects lost or stolen devices
- Secures online sessions with HTTPS
- Keeps backups and cloud files private
- Prevents attackers from reading sensitive information

### What you should do
- Enable **BitLocker** on Windows
- Turn on **FileVault** on macOS
- Use device encryption on smartphones
- Check for **HTTPS** before entering sensitive data
- Store important files in encrypted storage

Encryption keeps your digital life private, secure, and protected.
