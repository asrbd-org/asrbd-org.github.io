## 📧 Email Security – Protect Your Digital Gateway

> **Your email is the gateway to your digital life—are you protecting it?**

Email is one of the most important tools we use every day—but it’s also one of the most targeted by cybercriminals.

From phishing scams to account takeovers, attackers often use email as their first entry point. A single click on a malicious link or attachment can compromise your personal data, passwords, or even your entire digital identity.

Many attacks look legitimate—fake login pages, urgent messages, or emails pretending to be from trusted companies. This is how people get tricked.

Protecting your email doesn’t require advanced skills—just smart habits.

### 🛡️ How to Secure Your Email

- Do not click suspicious links or attachments  
- Always verify the sender’s email address  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)  
- Never reuse your email password on other sites  

> ⚠️ If your email is compromised, everything connected to it is at risk.

Stay cautious, stay informed, and protect your digital gateway.

---

### 📌 Key Takeaways

- Email is a primary attack vector  
- Phishing attacks are highly deceptive  
- One click can lead to major damage  
- Strong passwords + 2FA are essential  
- Awareness is your best defense
