## 📚 What is the ASRBD Knowledge Base?

In a rapidly evolving digital world, access to **reliable cybersecurity knowledge** is more important than ever. That’s why we are proud to introduce the **ASRBD Knowledge Base** — a dedicated repository designed to empower individuals, students, and professionals with structured, practical, and up-to-date cybersecurity knowledge.

This initiative is part of our mission to build a **safer digital Bangladesh** through awareness, education, and ethical cyber force development.

---

## 🎯 Why We Built It

Cyber threats are growing more sophisticated every day. From phishing attacks to ransomware, individuals and organizations face constant risks.

The ASRBD Knowledge Base was created to:

- Provide **free and accessible cybersecurity resources**
- Bridge the gap between **theoretical knowledge and real-world practice**
- Support **students and beginners** starting their cybersecurity journey
- Help build a **skilled and ethical cybersecurity community**

---

## 🧠 What You’ll Find

The Knowledge Base is designed to be **structured, practical, and beginner-friendly**, while also offering advanced insights.

### 🔰 Beginner Level
- Introduction to Cybersecurity
- Common Threats (Phishing, Malware, Social Engineering)
- Basic Digital Safety Practices

### 🛡️ Intermediate Level
- Network Security Fundamentals
- Web Application Security Basics
- Vulnerability Assessment Concepts

### ⚔️ Advanced Level
- Digital Forensics
- Ethical Hacking Methodologies
- Incident Response & Threat Analysis

### 🧰 Practical Resources
- Tools and Setup Guides
- Hands-on Labs (Coming Soon)
- Real-world Case Studies
