## 🚨 Think You’re Safe? These 7 Signs Say Otherwise

Most people don’t realize they’ve been hacked… until it’s too late.

Cyber attacks today are **silent, fast, and often invisible**. Hackers don’t always announce themselves—they observe, collect, and exploit quietly.

---

## ⚠️ 7 Warning Signs You Might Be Hacked

### 1. 🔔 Unusual Login Alerts
Getting login notifications from unknown locations or devices?

👉 If you didn’t log in — someone else did.

---

### 2. 🐢 Sudden Device Slowdown
Your phone or PC suddenly feels slow?

Malware or hidden processes may be running in the background.

---

### 3. 📩 Messages You Didn’t Send
Friends saying:
> “Why did you send me this?”

Your account may be spreading phishing links.

---

### 4. 🔑 Passwords Stop Working
If your password suddenly doesn’t work:

⚠️ It may have been changed by an attacker.

---

### 5. 🧾 Unknown Transactions
Seeing payments or subscriptions you didn’t make?

This indicates possible financial compromise.

---

### 6. 📲 New Apps or Extensions
Apps or browser extensions you didn’t install?

Attackers often use these to maintain access.

---

### 7. 🌐 Redirects & Popups
Strange websites, redirects, or excessive ads?

Your browser may be hijacked.

---

## 🚑 What To Do Immediately

If you notice any of these signs:

- Change all passwords (start with email)
- Enable 2FA (use authenticator apps)
- Run a full antivirus scan
- Log out from all devices
- Remove unknown apps or extensions
- Check recovery options (email/phone)

---

## 🛡️ Final Thought

Being hacked isn’t always obvious.

Sometimes, it’s **quiet, slow, and invisible**.

The earlier you detect it, the less damage it causes.
