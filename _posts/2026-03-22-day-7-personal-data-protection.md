## 🔐 Personal Data Protection – Protect Your Digital Identity

> **Your personal data is more valuable than you think—and hackers know it.**

Every time you sign up for a website, post on social media, or use an app, you leave behind digital footprints. This data—your name, email, phone number, location, and even your habits—can be collected, analyzed, and sometimes exploited.

Cybercriminals don’t always need to hack systems. Often, they gather publicly available information to target individuals. This is how identity theft, phishing attacks, and social engineering begin.

Protecting your personal data is not optional anymore—it’s essential.

### 🛡️ How to Protect Your Personal Data

- Limit what you share online  
- Review app permissions before installing  
- Use strong, unique passwords for every account  
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)  
- Be mindful of your digital footprint  

> ⚠️ Once your data is exposed, it’s extremely hard to take it back.

At **ASRBD**, we believe awareness is the first step toward security. Stay mindful, stay informed, and take control of your digital identity.

---

### 📌 Key Takeaways

- Personal data is a prime target for cybercriminals  
- Oversharing increases risk  
- Always review permissions  
- Strong passwords + 2FA are essential  
- Your digital footprint matters
