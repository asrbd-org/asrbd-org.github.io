## 🔐 Secure Wi-Fi & VPN

⚠️ **Free Wi-Fi can cost you your privacy.**

Public Wi-Fi is everywhere—cafes, airports, hotels—but most people don’t realize how dangerous it can be. When you connect to an open network, your data can be intercepted by attackers using simple tools. This is known as a **Man-in-the-Middle attack**, where hackers secretly monitor your online activity.

Without protection, your passwords, emails, and even banking information can be exposed.

### 🛡️ Why Use a VPN?

A **VPN (Virtual Private Network)** encrypts your internet traffic and creates a secure tunnel between your device and the internet. Even if someone tries to intercept your data, they will only see unreadable encrypted information.

### 🔑 How to Stay Safe

- Avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi  
- Always use a trusted VPN service  
- Turn off auto-connect to unknown networks  
- Only browse websites with **HTTPS**  
- Verify network names before connecting  

### ⚡ Key Takeaways

- Public Wi-Fi is not secure  
- Hackers can easily intercept unprotected data  
- VPN adds a critical layer of encryption  
- Safe browsing habits reduce risk significantly
