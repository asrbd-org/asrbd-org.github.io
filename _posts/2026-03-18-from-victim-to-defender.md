# 🚨 It Starts With a Click

You receive a message.

> “Your account will be locked. Verify now.”

You click.

Within minutes — your account is gone.

This is the reality of modern cyber attacks.

They don’t start with complex code.  
They start with **human behavior**.

---

# 🧠 What Hacking Really Means

Hacking is often misunderstood.

It’s not just:
- Writing complex code  
- Breaking systems instantly  

Most attacks rely on:
- **Social engineering**
- **Psychological manipulation**
- **Weak security habits**

---

# ⚙️ The 5 Stages of a Cyber Attack

Understanding these stages can help you defend yourself.

## 🔍 1. Reconnaissance
Attackers gather information:
- Emails  
- Social media  
- Personal data  

---

## 📡 2. Scanning
They identify weaknesses:
- Weak passwords  
- Outdated systems  
- Exposed accounts  

---

## 🔓 3. Gaining Access
Common methods include:
- Phishing  
- Malware  
- Credential stuffing  

---

## 🕳️ 4. Maintaining Access
Attackers secure control:
- Change credentials  
- Add recovery methods  
- Create backdoors  

---

## 🧹 5. Covering Tracks
They hide evidence:
- Delete logs  
- Mask identity  
- Remove alerts  

---

# 🎭 Real Example: Facebook Account Hack

A typical attack looks like this:

1. You receive a fake login alert  
2. You click a phishing link  
3. You enter your credentials  
4. Attacker logs in instantly  
5. Your account is locked  

---

# 🛡️ How to Protect Yourself

Take action now:

- 🔐 Use strong, unique passwords  
- 🔒 Enable 2FA  
- 🌐 Check URLs carefully  
- ⚠️ Avoid suspicious links  
- 📱 Keep software updated
