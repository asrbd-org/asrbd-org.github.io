## 🆓 Top Free Cybersecurity Resources

Cybersecurity education doesn’t have to be expensive. In fact, some of the **best learning platforms, tools, and communities are completely free**.

At ASRBD, we believe that **financial limitations should never stop anyone from learning cybersecurity**. This guide highlights the best free resources to help you start and grow your journey.

---

## 🎓 Free Learning Platforms

### 🧠 Interactive Learning

- **TryHackMe**  
  Beginner-friendly platform with guided paths and hands-on labs.

- **Hack The Box (HTB)**  
  More advanced, real-world hacking challenges and labs.

- **PortSwigger Web Security Academy**  
  Best resource for learning web vulnerabilities like XSS, SQL Injection.

---

## 💡 Tips to Use Free Resources Effectively

- Focus on **learning path, not random content**  
- Practice daily, even 1–2 hours  
- Take notes and document your journey  
- Build a home lab  
- Don’t rely only on videos — **practice is key**
