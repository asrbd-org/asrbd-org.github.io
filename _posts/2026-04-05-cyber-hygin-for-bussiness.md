## 🔐 Cyber Hygiene for Your Business

Cybersecurity is no longer optional.

One weak point can expose your entire business.

---

## ⚠️ What is Cyber Hygiene?

Cyber hygiene means maintaining simple, consistent security practices to protect systems and data.

---

## 🧾 Essential Checklist

### 🔄 Keep Systems Updated
- Update OS and software  
- Patch vulnerabilities  
- Remove outdated tools  

---

### 🔐 Use Strong Passwords
- Long and unique passwords  
- No reuse  
- Use password managers  

---

### 🎯 Train Employees
- Identify phishing  
- Avoid suspicious links  
- Follow safe practices  

---

### 💾 Maintain Backups
- Keep offline backups  
- Test regularly  
- Store securely  

---

### 🔑 Enable 2FA
- Use authenticator apps  
- Secure critical systems  

---

### 🧪 Monitor Systems
- Track logins  
- Review access  
- Perform audits  

---

## ⚠️ Reality

Small businesses are common targets due to weak security.

---

## 🧠 Final Thoughts

Cybersecurity is about consistency.

Simple steps can prevent major damage.
